Glow Up Blog

WHY I Created This Website

It all began with a small anxiety and depression test conducted in school by the psychology department. When the results came out, a majority of us—including me—were silently suffering from anxiety. It made me realise how deeply our mental health is being ignored in our everyday lives.

I’ve always been a sensitive soul, deeply in tune with my emotions. This space was born from that feeling. A safe corner of the internet where women could come together, reflect, heal, and glow up—not just outside, but from within.

My Glow Up Journey

I used to label myself as "average." I believed I wasn't special, wasn’t smart enough, not beautiful enough. But somewhere along the way, I shifted my mindset. I began saying, "I’m not average; I’m learning. I’m growing. I’m becoming HER."

My glow-up wasn’t overnight. It took tears, journaling, boundary setting, 4 AM wake-ups, and believing in myself when no one else did. This space exists because I know how lonely that journey can be, and I want to walk it with you.

Realistic & Scientific Glow Up Tips

  • 🧠 Practice gratitude daily – proven to increase happiness.
  • 🛏️ Sleep 7–9 hours – your brain and skin need it.
  • 💧 Hydrate & eat whole foods – glowing starts from inside.
  • 🧘‍♀️ Move your body – even 15 minutes a day makes a difference.
  • 📖 Read, learn, evolve – become obsessed with your growth.
  • 🚫 Avoid toxic comparison – social media isn't real life.
