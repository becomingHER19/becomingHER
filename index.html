About HER

I'm a 16-year-old South Indian girl building my dream life from scratch— one journal page, one study session, one vision board at a time.

*becomingHER* is more than a website—it's a movement. A space where girls like me, from normal families with not-so-normal dreams, find growth, self-love, and unapologetic confidence. You're safe here. You're seen here. And you're never too young to become THAT girl.
